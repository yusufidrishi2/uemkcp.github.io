ABOUT US

Who We Are!
In one word, Aspring Competitive Coders who one day dream to represent our college UEMK in ACM-ICPC. We code and help others to code. We organise events, workshops and host competitions on Codechef solely for students of our campus to make them aware about the nitty gritties of Competitive Coding. To those who are new to this term, Competitive programming is a mind sport usually held over the Internet or a local network, involving participants trying to program according to provided specifications. Contestants are referred to as sport programmers. Competitive programming is recognized and supported by several multinational software and Internet companies, such as Google, Amazon and Facebook.There are several organizations who host programming competitions on a regular basis. Through competitive programming we seek to enhance our problem solving abilites and analytical skills to crack coding interviews in placement interviews.

OUR COLLEGE UEMK


UEM Kolkata is established in the year 2014 by Act No 25 of 2014 of Govt of West Bengal. Being located in New Town, the Smart City of Eastern India and very near to the Netaji Subhash International Airport, the students of the University are exposed to the top corporates. Sites To Practice From

"Knowledge is of no value unless you put it into practice."

Anton Chekhov

CodeChef is a non-profit educational initiative of Directi. It is a global competitive programming platform which supports over 50 programming languages and has a large community of programmers that helps students and professionals test and improve their coding skills.

SPOJ (Sphere Online Judge) is an online judge system with over 315,000 registered users and over 20,000 problems. Tasks are prepared by its community of problem setters or are taken from previous programming contests. SPOJ allows advanced users to organize contests under their own rules and also includes a forum where programmers can discuss how to solve a particular problem.

HackerRank is a technology company that focuses on competitive programming challenges for both consumers and businesses, where developers compete by trying to program according to provided specifications. HackerRank's programming challenges can be solved in a variety of programming languages (including Java, C++, PHP, Python, SQL, JavaScript) and span multiple computer science domains.

HackerEarth is an India-based popular meeting ground for budding programmers and developers.It is a global hub of 1.5 million developers. For enterprises, HackerEarth is a software provider for innovation management & technical recruitment. HackerEarth is very useful for beginners due to its exclusive programming tutorial provided by Code-Monk. It also has classified problem which can be very useful for practicing.

Topcoder is a company that administers contests in computer programming. Topcoder hosts fortnightly online competitive programming competitions—known as SRMs or "single round matches"—as well as weekly competitions in graphic design and development. The work in design and development produces useful software which is licensed for profit by Topcoder.

Codeforces is a social network dedicated to programming and programming contests. Also, it is a platform where contests are held regularly, the participant's skills are reflected by their rating and the former contests can be used to prepare. Codeforces constantly develops and we plan to improve the platform to give the participants the opportunity to organize their own contests, filling the project with learning content, developing Codeforces as a training and learning platform.
